We have 22 HP between our hands, and opponents with only 18 points between them managed to find an opening and an invite. Their points must be splitting 9-9 or 8-10 and they must have good suit quality and distribution to justify all the bidding with so few points. At matchpoints the double of 3!s could be from just three spades, but considering they lack any power in high cards and their implied distribution spades must be 4-1. If he chooses to keep three diamonds and one heart we can play our heart overtaking with the A and cash the now good 9 of hearts, if he chooses to keep 3 hearts and stiff Ace of diamonds we can just duck a diamond too the A and collect the next 3 tricks. If he chooses to keep 2 cards in both red suits we have two options 1) play heart to the Ace and and an other heart end-playing East into giving us the K of diamonds 2) cash the King of hearts and give up a diamond, East will cash his second diamond but will have to concede the last trick to dummies Ace of hearts.
