club bids are natural.
double is balanced, or clubs with the unbid majors

bidding spades shows a spades (could be just 5 cards)

2x Openings

Description In third seat the opening are wide ranging (both in shape and range)
First and second seat vulnerable the opening are fairlly standard weak two, agressive but tend to always have a 6 card suit
First and second seat not vulnerable the openings show 7 to 10 points and a five card or longer suit. We tend to almost never upgrade 5/6 counts into this opening.
With a 6 card suit with no side suit honors we prefer to open at the 3 level, so that that a not vulnerable 2 level opening is either from a 5 card suit or has at least a side suit Q
This is important as the style is for partner to agressively look to penalize oppenents (so do not be tempeted to open xx KQJxxx xxx xx at the two level)
They tend to deny 4 card majors (unless in a 6/4 with a good primary suit and bad major).
Not vulnerable there is no further requirment on the opening, the suit could be Jxxxx, and it could be from a 5332 distribution
