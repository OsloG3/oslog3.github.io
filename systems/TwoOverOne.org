**Grue switch**\\

When opponents show !s and partner bids a natural 3!h and no other suits have been bid. Like: (2!s) - 3!h - (pass) - ? now 3!s is a stopper ask or a strong natural 4!c bid, while 4!c shows a !h raise with stronger than 4!h\\

**Other switch:**\\

When we have shown a major and opponents compete at the 3 level or partner has just competed to 3M and we don't have a 3level cuebid we use 4!c as the strong raise, and the cuebid as the natural 4!c bid\\

Examples: \\

1M-(3!d/3oM)- ? 4!c=stronger raise; 4cue = strong !c.\\

(3!d)-3M-(pass)-? \\

(2!s) - 3!h  - (pass) - ?\\
