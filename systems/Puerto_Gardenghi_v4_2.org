* Carding

Standard count and reverse attitude. K lead asks for count(or unblock vs NT), the Q could be from a weak KQx(x) holding \\

vs Suit:\\

3/low leads, suit preference in the trump suit\\

vs NT:\\

2/4: low from small dubleton( 9x we lead x, 10x we lead the 10), the 9 promises exatcly 1 higher card( that could be the 10)\\

3/5: if partner showed 4+cards in the suit\\

reverse smith (low I like the lead suit) only at trick 2 after declarer won the opening lead. Smith is off ONLY if declarer plays a suit where we might need to cutoff dummy( dummy has no side entry or only 1 and the suit requires 2 to setup). In all other cases Smith is on. If the lead suit has a clear layout giving encoureging smith shows that we don’t like the switch to new suit.\\

*** Key card asking

RKCB with suit X set:

- step-1: 1 or 4 KC
- step-2: 0 or 3 KC
- step-3: 2 + no Q of trumps
- step-4: 2 + Q of trumps
- step-5: even KC + a void
- step-6+: odd KC + a void in bid suit/ in a suit above if bidding the trump suit

After step 1 and 2 the first next available step that isn’t 5X asks for the Q of trumps.\\

If the Q ask is below 5X then: 5X = no Q; 6X = Q + new kings to bid, bid in between Q + the king in the suit bid.\\

If the Q ask is above 5X it promises all KC then: 

- 6X = no Q;
- 5NT = Q no King
- new suit = Q + King in the suit bid
- the cheapest bid is ambiguos a) the meaning given by the rules above b) Q + a king that cant be bid below 6X and no other Kings.

After steps 1 to 4 the first available step that isn’ the trump suit, or the Q ask, is a specific king ask and promises all KC.\\

We bid the the cheapest suit we have a King and if available can use 5NT to show a king above 6X.\\

After a positive response to the King or Q ask bidding a suit where partner could  still have a king ask for that specific king.\\

Bidding a suit where partner cannot have a King asks for 3rd round control in that suit.\\

Bidding 5NT, either directly after RKCB(if it is not a Q or K ask) or after a positive Q or K ask response asks for specific Queens: we bid the first Q we have available to bid.\\

If we have denied a King in suit(either we skipped the cubdbid, or partner cuebid the suit and is known to not have shortness in said suit and we have the A in that suit) we treat the Q in that suit as a King in response to the Q or K ask (but still a Q if partner makes a 5NT specific Q ask)\\
